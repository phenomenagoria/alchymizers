How to Play

Goal

Earn the most Reputation over 9 rounds of moonshine distilling. The distiller with the highest reputation wins!

Round Flow

  1. Holler Card — A fortune event affects everyone.
  2. Brew Phase — Draw ingredients from your stash one at a time. Each ingredient moves you along the proof track. Stop anytime to lock in your score, or push further for bigger rewards. But watch the pressure gauge...
  3. Blowout! — If Bad Mash pressure exceeds 7, your still explodes! You choose to keep either the dollars or the reputation from your position, but not both.
  4. Market — Spend dollars to buy new ingredients for your stash (up to 2 different types per round).

Ingredients

  • Bad Mash — Adds to still pressure. Too much = blowout!
  • Corn — Reliable base. Moves you along the track.
  • Juniper — If last or second-last drawn, gain 1 copper.
  • Charcoal — Bonus movement equal to corn already placed.
  • Copper Coil — Draws a bonus ingredient. If it's Bad Mash, it gets filtered out!
  • Sugar — +1 bonus movement for each sugar already placed.
  • Ghost Pepper — End-of-round bonuses based on how many you placed.

Copper

Earn copper by landing on prime-numbered spaces on the track. Spend 2 copper during the market phase to advance your flame start position by 1 — you'll begin each round further along the track!

Winning

After 9 rounds, the player with the most reputation wins. Ties broken by track position.
